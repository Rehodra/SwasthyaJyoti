Help Centre

Find quick answers to common questions below.

How do I register a new person?

Go to the Register page, verify OTP, and fill in personal details to create a profile.

How do I generate a QR code?

Navigate to the Generate QR section under Profile and click on "Download QR".

What is disease prediction %?

This indicates the predicted spread of communicable diseases among registered arrivals in each state.
